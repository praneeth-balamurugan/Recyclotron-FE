About Us,

 Growing population and demand creates need for manufacturing of food and equivalently its disposal. Even though it has been biodegradable it is put to just manuring or composting uses. Value addition to waste is the concept of our web application. Connecting different food and bio waste producers to the right users can earn profits and proper utilization of resources to its maximum. The wastes from restaurants and other Agricultural industries are collected as raw materials. Without separation are used as feed for a Pest called Black Soldier Fly (BSF). Later this BSF can be used as feed for poultry, as it consumes waste and converts the waste directly to animal protein. There's not much awareness about this beneficial pest in Tamil Nadu. Utilization of waste this way reduces time and more complex processing.
